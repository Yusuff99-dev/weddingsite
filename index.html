Travel Tips

You can get from Melbourne Airport to the City either by car or bus, the SkyBus is $20 each way and runs direct from the airport to Southern Cross Station in the middle of Melbourne City. There are also Uber/Taxi ranks at the airport for around $70-100 depending on your time of arrival.

St John's Anglican Church is on Toorak Road, which runs straight through to The Royce, St Kilda, you can either hop in a Taxi/Uber or take the 58 West-Coburg - Toorak tram and get off at Toorak Rd/St Kilda Rd stop #22.
